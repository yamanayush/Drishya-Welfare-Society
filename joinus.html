Join The Drishya WELFARE Team


Drishya welfare partners with potential organizations offering programs in the development sector. As a partner non-profit, Drishya Welfare provides opportunities to work closely at the grassroots with the communities to bring social change. It gives them the space to turn their ideas into actions as they propose, design and implements their projects across the different thematic areas where the organization works.



If you are a driven individual and a team player who can support us in achieving our organization’s vision and goals, join us to become a part of one of the largest movements.



To join the Drishya Welfare Society family, register yourself through the button given below.


Internship


Your internship experience with us will help you develop invaluable professional skills and build your leadership capabilities. As an intern, you will have the opportunity to take up a valued role within Drishya Welfare Society, and contribute to one of the largest movements.



Why Intern With Drishya Welfare Society?


An internship at Drishya Welfare Society is an opportunity to gain experience on diverse activities, honing skills and understanding the functionalities of a grassroots organisation while working with an experienced and diverse team of professionals. Our eclectic group of interns comprises youngsters from educational institutions throughout India and abroad. Working as an intern with Drishya Welfare Society gives you a chance to connect at the grassroots; understand the various issues, and contribute to achieving the goals.
