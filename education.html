EDUCATION


Drishya Welfare Society understands the true meaning of education and the need for the same. Having the understanding that children are the future of Bharat. And are concerned about that. We provide remote classes for those you are deprived from education.


Initiatives which society is finding very useful:



Free Book Bank



We are privileged to provide free books for the Children who are deprived from facilities, over 500+ books have been distributed to the needy ones. 100s of children who want to study, but the lack of resources ceases their ambitious growth. Drishya welfare society gives away all the books they got from donation camps. People of Ratlam city are putting their step forward for such children.

Admissions and classes



Drishya provides admissions facility for children who have no resource for joining schools, along with free classes. Slum areas has a very high number of children who dreams of going school but couldn’t. We introduce government policies to them, help them getting the resources.

Drishya Welfare Society is a Ratlam based non-profit organization (NGO) Registered under the Societies Registration Act, 1860.
