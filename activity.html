Trending Activities

Education- Free Book Bank

We are privileged to provide free books for the Children who are deprived from facilities, over 500+ books have been distributed to the needy ones. 100s of children who want to study, but the lack of resources ceases their ambitious growth. Drishya welfare society gives away all the books they got from donation camps. People of Ratlam city are putting their step forward for such children.

Education- Admission And Class

Drishya provides admissions facility for children who have no resource for joining schools, along with free classes. Slum areas has a very high number of children who dreams of going school but could not. We introduce government policies to them, help them getting the resources.

Protechting Reserviors- Pond Conservation

Jhali talab in Ratlam is a beautiful place where one can find peace. Deposition of garbage and plastic bags made the whole lake an awful place. Fishes started dying. This all made us stand for a cause to save our beloved water reservoirs. Team Drishya cleaned it and did all the things for water purification. With higher authorities to provide garbage containers near Jhali talab.

Protechting Reserviors-
River conservation

Jaman river is one of the cases. Being situated in Ratlam outskirt, it is a perfect picnic spot. And what else could happen when a lot of people comes and enjoy their day. The river started polluting. It was pathetic to see this beautiful place in this situation.
Team Drishya took the stand to clean the river and make it again pollution free by giving 100 % effort for our beloved Jaman river. We also appealed to he people, to have concern and cleaning measures for rivers.

Reviving Stepwells (Bavadi)

Stepwells (Bavadi) near Unkala temple, Ratlam. were in endangered condition. Drishya pledge to clean it and conserve it as well. And giving continues 7 hours efforts from 30 members made that possible. These Stepwells (Bavadi) now days has become a garbage pit. And as a responsible citizen we are here to bring back the culture and pride of our historic monuments.

Reviving old gardens

Revived old gardens with the help of locals living there. Gifting plant saplings with a message to plant more and more trees

Health through medicinal plants

Distribution of 1000+ Giloy stems, Basil (Tulsi) Plant and other herbs for immunity booster.

Plantation

Drishya welfare society emphasis on GO Green Message, and works promoting nature. Thereby, planting 5000+ plants and herbs in hilly areas, and successful in Sowing Dates, Babool, Subbul, Karanj in rocky fields of Jaman river, Ratlam.

Street Act Location

Awareness campaign with street act done by Drishya Welfare society. Fun activity and several community awareness programs conducted by Drishya welfare Society.

Honouring Corona warriors Location

During and post lockdown Drishya welfare society was privileged to honour corona warries.

Kerala Flood donation Location

On 8 August 2019, due to heavy rainfall in the Monsoon season, severe flood that issued Red alert in the 9 districts in Northern and Central Kerala. Drishya welfare society initiated a cloth donation camp. Donating over 100+ boxes of cloths and other essentials requirements.

Drishya Welfare Society is a Ratlam based non-profit organization (NGO) Registered under the Societies Registration Act, 1860.
