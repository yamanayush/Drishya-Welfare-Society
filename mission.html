Mission


Drishya Welfare Society has the following mission:

❖ To help create people’s organization and community groups which work for the development of their members and their localities.
❖ To interact and partner with various government and non-government service providers to enhance provision of services and their access to community.
❖ To restore, preserve and develop the natural bio-diversity.
❖ Planting more and more trees and taking initiative for water source conservation.

Drishya Welfare Society is a Ratlam based non-profit organization (NGO) Registered under the Societies Registration Act, 1860.
